Clown-Swing


< Back       Next >


Clown-Swing is a 3D diorama made in Maya with textures generated in Substance Designer. This project was made to explore dramatic lighting in a 3D space as well as modelling and texturing in a "Hyper-Realistic" style akin to that of a modern survival horror game.
